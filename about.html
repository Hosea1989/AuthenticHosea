Background Story

Damien's journey in the tech world began in 2016 when he decided to return to school, majoring in Computer Science at Los Angeles City College. His fascination with video games, technology, and tinkering led him to pursue a career in software development.

Now, as an Educator and Community Based Organizer for the Hidden Genius Project, Damien trains and mentors black male youth in technology creation, entrepreneurship, and leadership skills.
